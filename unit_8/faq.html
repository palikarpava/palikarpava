Polls are a quick and interactive way to ask a single question of your website visitors. You can also choose to display realtime results and location to your respondents!

There are 2 ways you can start a new poll

Polls are a quick and interactive way to ask a single question of your website visitors. You can also choose to display realtime results and location to your respondents!

  • Click the ‘Polls’ tab at the left side in the admin main panel and click on ‘Add New’
  • Click on ‘Add New’ button in the Polls panel

Add question

Make sure your question is easy to read and understand for your own tracking purposes.

  • Give your poll a name, it will appear in the dashboard in the future
  • Type in the question

Assign your poll to the category

Categories help to create powerful reference for the user.

  • Check the ‘Category Name’ you want your poll to be in
  • You can check more than one category to display your poll

Set feature image

Feature image will be displayed at a cover for your poll or it can be the additional content to visually enhance your poll

  • Click on the "Set featured image" link
  • In your Media Library select image for your poll
  • Click on ‘Set featured image’ button

Working with Choices

When you create choices that the user will choose from, don't forget to stay creative: text or image.

  • Select the type of choices you will work with: ‘Text’ or ‘Image’
  • Type in the question
  • If you would like your image appear with your choices, check ‘Display featured image along with the choices’ checkbox
  • If you would like featured image stay hidden — leave it empty
  • Type in the answer that user will see to the ‘label’ field
  • Your answer could be as simple as 'Yes' or as complicated as a full quote from a famous writer
  • When you would like to create second choice, click on the ‘Text’ or ‘Image’ again

Add Value Proposition to the Choice

Value proposition can be primary or additional source of your business model, don't forget to relate your user results to why a consumer should buy a product or use a service.

  • Add value proposition message based on the choice
  • Add value proposition URL based to the same choice

Add Follow up questions to the Choice

Follow up questions is a great method to gain additional insight to user's decision making process.

  • Add Follow Up question to the same choice

Add Thank You Message to the Choice

The small act of saying “thank you” shows prospects, customers and others you really care about them, not just their input.

  • Add Follow Up question to the same choice
  • Add the text you would like to be visible to the users
  • If you would like to add media, select ‘Add Media’ button

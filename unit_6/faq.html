Polls are a quick and interactive way to ask a single question of your website visitors. You can also choose to display realtime results and location to your respondents!

There are 2 ways you can start

Add New Poll: Click the ‘Polls’ tab at the left side in the admin main panel and click on ‘Add New’ button in the Polls panel

Question:

Give your Poll a Name, it will appear in the dashboard in the future Type in the question

Categories:

Check the ‘Category Name’ you want your Poll to be in
You can check more than one category to display your poll

Set Featured Image:

Click on the ‘Set featured image’ link

In your Media Library select image for your Poll

Click ‘Set featured image’ button

Choices:

You can add as many choices as you like, you can add redirect links, custom thank you message, add photos and simply use text
